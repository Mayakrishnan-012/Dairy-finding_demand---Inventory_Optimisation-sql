# 📊 Demand Forecasting and Inventory Optimization in the Dairy Industry

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **demand patterns and inventory behavior** in the dairy manufacturing industry.  
The goal is to identify factors affecting **sales performance, shelf life, and product wastage**, and propose **data-driven strategies for improving inventory and distribution decisions**.

The analysis focuses on understanding how **product demand, storage conditions, sales channels, and regional factors** influence dairy product performance.

---

## ⚠️ Business Problem
Dairy products are **highly perishable**, requiring efficient inventory planning.  
Poor demand forecasting and inefficient distribution can lead to:

- ❌ Product wastage due to expiry  
- 💸 Revenue loss  
- 📦 Overstocking or stock shortages  
- 🚚 Inefficient channel allocation  

This project demonstrates how **data analytics can optimize inventory replenishment and distribution strategies**.

---

## 🔎 Key Analytical Areas

### 📈 Revenue Realization
Evaluates how effectively produced inventory converts into **actual revenue** across brands, locations, and sales channels.

### 📊 Sell-Through Rate
Measures the **efficiency of inventory movement** by analyzing how much of the produced quantity is successfully sold.

### ⏳ Shelf-Life Analysis
Examines how **storage conditions and product characteristics** influence shelf life and inventory risk.

### ♻️ Wastage Analysis
Identifies **products and operational conditions** that contribute to higher inventory wastage.

---

## ⚙️ Operational Insights
Based on analytical results, the project introduces **rule-based recommendations** for:

- 📦 Inventory reorder optimization  
- 🚚 Distribution channel allocation  
- ❄️ Storage condition improvements  
- ♻️ Wastage reduction strategies  

These insights support **better supply chain decision-making**.
